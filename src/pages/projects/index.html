环球融创曹山未来城

坐落在地处沪、宁、杭以及苏锡常4大都市圈交汇之心的江苏溧阳曹山，拥有18万平方公里水域、5公里黄金水岸线、56座岛屿。葱郁森林，密布湖泊， 氤氲温泉等丰富景观资源，助其坐拥整个长三角广阔的文旅消费市场。选址曹山，环球融创意图在这里建造一座悬浮于山、湖、谷、森林、岛屿之上的14000亩超级未来度假城市， 曹山未来城。

如此大体量的文旅度假项目，仅展示区面积就达22万㎡，覆盖销售中心、艺术中心、汤屋民宿、户外体验等多重功能。4万㎡的水体面积， 场地茂密的原始松林为景观设计奠定了基底，也成了设计最重要的主题。展示区地形从西面主入口到岸边一共跌落约12m高差，湖岸往东再回升约7m，形成以湖面为最低点、 两边高中间低的谷地地形。我们的设计依托在地自然资源，以“水”为题，展开这一方江南温婉水乡的设计，流动的水系串联东西两侧湖体、自然风光和景观节点， 营造水岸生活的度假感与体验感。在保留原有自然肌理的前提下，挖掘属于场地本身的记忆，并创造新的建筑景观记忆点。

Located in Caoshan, Liyang, Jiangsu, which is located at the intersection of the four metropolitan areas of Shanghai, Nanjing, Hangzhou and Suzhou, Wuxi and Changzhou, it has 180,000 square kilometers of water, 5 kilometers of golden waterfront, and 56 islands. Rich landscape resources such as lush forests, dense lakes, and hot springs help it to sit on the vast cultural tourism consumer market in the entire Yangtze River Delta. Caoshan is selected as the site, and Global Finance Creative Map is here to build a super futuristic resort city of 14,000 acres suspended above mountains, lakes, valleys, forests and islands, Caoshan Future City.

Such a large-scale cultural tourism and vacation project has an area of ​​220,000 square meters in the exhibition area alone, covering multiple functions such as sales center, art center, soup house, outdoor experience and so on. The water area of ​​40,000 square meters and the dense original pine forest on the site laid the foundation for the landscape design and became the most important theme of the design. The terrain of the exhibition area falls from the main entrance on the west side to the shore by a total height difference of about 12m, and the lake shore rises to the east by about 7m, forming a valley terrain with the lake as the lowest point and the high and the middle low on both sides. Our design relies on local natural resources and takes "water" as the theme to unfold the design of the Jiangnan Wenwan Water Village. The flowing water system connects the lakes, natural scenery and landscape nodes on both sides of the east and west, creating a sense of resort and life on the waterfront. Sense of experience. On the premise of preserving the original natural texture, excavate the memory belonging to the site itself and create new memory points of the architectural landscape.
