此处为甲方简单信息，坐落在地处沪、宁、杭以及苏锡常4大都市圈交汇之心的江苏溧阳曹山，拥有18万平方公里水域、5公里黄金水岸线、 56座岛屿。葱郁森林，密布湖泊，氤氲温泉等丰富景观资源，助其坐拥整个长三角广阔的文旅消费市场。

Located in Caoshan, Liyang, Jiangsu, which is located at the intersection of the four metropolitan areas of Shanghai, Nanjing, Hangzhou, Suzhou, Wuxi and Changzhou, it has 180,000 square kilometers of water, 5 kilometers of golden waterfront, and 56 islands. Rich landscape resources such as lush forests, dense lakes, and hot springs help it to sit on the vast cultural tourism consumer market in the entire Yangtze River Delta.
